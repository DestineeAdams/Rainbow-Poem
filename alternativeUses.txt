Alternative uses/Bed

My bed is meant for sleeping.
But sometimes I work on it, hunched over my laptop in deep focus.

My bed is meant for sleeping.
But sometimes I eat on it, painstakingly making sure I don't drop a crumb.

My bed is meant for sleeping.
But sometimes I cry on it, sobbing softly into the sheets, my arms wrapped around my pillow.

My bed is meant for sleeping.
But sometimes I relax on it, arms behind my head eyes to the ceiling listening to morning Doves

So many uses. Alternative uses
